Cheese Enchiladas

Cheese wrapped in delcious tortillas...topped with more cheese! - ummm...cheese.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons taco seasoning
  • 1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced ripe olives
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Warm tortillas in microwave or in an oven. Dip warmed tortillas in tomato sauce
    mixture and lay them in a casserole dish. Fill each tortilla with cheese, diced onions,
    olives and mushrooms. Roll the tortillas carefully. Repeat until dish is full. Sprinkle
    good amount of cheese on top.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
